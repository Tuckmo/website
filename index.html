Biography

I am a recent graduate from Macquarie University, where I studied a Bachelor of Information Technology - Major in Web Design and Development. Previously I have studied at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment, where I received a Advanced Diploma In Professional Game Development - Specialising in Art. Acquiring these 2 certifications have given me a wide range of skills across digital media, from creating websites(front end and back end), to manipulating databases, creating 3D models and video games.

From a young age I have had strong interest in computers, and have continually striven to include technology as apart of my future career. This has presented itself in my studies of IT and games design. In the past I have been briefly contracted by ICMS to create forms for their enrollment process, taught a Certificate III in Game Design Foundations and have also interned at Epiphany Games as a 3D prop model artist.

My main draw into technology has been the process of solving problems. I love the challenge of researching and finding an answer to problems that arise, whether it is a technical art problem, bugs in code or simply troubleshooting for family members or friends when their computers break down or start acting weird.
